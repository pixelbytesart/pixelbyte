Who We Are

At Ewelina's Dog Boarding Services, we are dedicated with a passion for providing exceptional care to your furry family members. With years of experience in the pet care industry, we understand the importance of creating a loving and secure environment for your dogs.

Our mission is to ensure that every tail wags with joy and every pet owner feels confident in the well-being of their beloved companions. From our cozy boarding facilities to personalized dog walks and attentive pet sitting services, we go the extra mile to make sure your pets receive the care they deserve.

Services

At Ewelina's Dog Boarding Services, we understand that every dog is unique, and that's why we offer a range of specialized services to cater to their individual needs.

Whether your furry friend needs a cozy place to stay, an invigorating walk, or the comfort of home while you're away, we've got you covered.

Dog Boarding

Give your pup a home away from home! Our boarding facilities are designed to provide a safe and comfortable environment for your dogs. With spacious play areas and cozy resting spaces, we ensure your pets enjoy a vacation of their own.

Dog Walking

Exercise is essential for a happy and healthy dog. Our experienced dog walkers are dedicated to providing engaging walks tailored to your pet's energy levels. From leisurely strolls to energetic adventures, we've got the perfect walk for every dog.

Pet Sitting

For those who prefer the comfort of their own space, our pet sitting services offer personalized care in familiar surroundings. Our professional sitters ensure your pets receive the attention, love, and routine they deserve while you're away.
